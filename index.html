HemAI

HemAI is a collection of publicly available and free machine learning models for the diagnosis of various rare hematology diseases using routine biological parameters.

Thrombotic Thrombocytopenia Purpura (TTP)

TTP is a rare, life-threatening blood disorder characterized by the formation of microscopic blood clots throughout the body that consume platelets, damage red blood cells, and restrict blood flow to vital organs like the brain and kidneys.

Paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria (PNH)

PNH is a rare, acquired, and life-threatening blood disease characterized by the premature destruction of red blood cells by the body's own immune system, leading to anemia, blood clots, and bone marrow failure.
